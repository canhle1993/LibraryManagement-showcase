# Admin Features

The Library Management System gives administrators visibility and control over the full internal operation of the library.

## Main Admin Modules

- Book management
- Bibliographic data management for authors, publishers, and categories
- Warehouse and stock management
- Location management
- Personnel approval and status management
- Borrow history tracking
- Damage management
- Recycle bin management
- Dashboard charts and reports

## Key Admin Capabilities

- approve or reject pending staff accounts
- manage books, descriptions, prices, rental prices, and stock
- assign and organize storage locations
- track damaged books and repair costs
- restore soft-deleted records or permanently remove them
- monitor revenue and borrowing statistics

## Why This Matters

This makes the project more than a simple CRUD app. It behaves like an operational back-office tool for a real library workflow.
